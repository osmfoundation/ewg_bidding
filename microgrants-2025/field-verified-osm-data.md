## 1. Scope of Work
The Grantee agrees to undertake the following tasks:  
- Try to better include the OSM Community in defining **what** field data is valuable to collect, and to what OSM tags they should be matched. Via community forums.
- Define XLSForm surveys for field data collection of different OSM feature types.
- Integrate these surveys into the FieldTM project creator, allowing projects to be generated in downstream tools (ODK + QField for now, with future scope for EveryDoor, ChatMap, Kobo, and others).
- Test the forms in the field with trusted partners at OSM Sierra Leone to further iterate.

## 2. Timeline & Milestones
The Grantee commits to the following schedule (full breakdown in linked GH discussion)
- **Milestone 1:** Gather requirements from community — Due end Feb 2026. 
- **Milestone 2:** Implementation and testing of new form category types — Due end March 2026.

- **Final Deliverable:** a working solution for field mapping various OSM categories — Due June 2026.
