## 1. Scope of Work
The Grantee agrees to carry out the following tasks as part of the project:

- **Detailed Implementation Plan**  
  Preparation of a comprehensive technical and operational plan outlining the approach, workflow, requirements, and resource allocation.

- **Alpha Release of the Library**  
  Delivery of an initial working version of the library with core functionalities implemented.

- **Feedback Integration, Infrastructure Setup, and Production Release**  
  Iterative improvements based on feedback, deployment infrastructure preparation, and release of the production-ready version.

- **Public Launch & Final Reporting**  
  Public demonstration of the final product at **SOTM 2026 (Paris)** and submission of the final project report.
