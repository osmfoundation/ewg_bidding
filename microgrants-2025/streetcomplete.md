## 1. Scope of Work
The Grantee agrees to undertake the following tasks:  

1. Notify users of upcoming events from [OSM calendar](https://osmcal.org/) in their vicinity ([#1798](https://github.com/streetcomplete/StreetComplete/issues/1798))

2. Make use of the [osm-community-index](https://github.com/osmlab/osm-community-index/) to direct users to local community channels based on their geo-location ([#1797](https://github.com/streetcomplete/StreetComplete/issues/1797))

3. Notify users of a new edition of [weeklyOSM](https://weeklyosm.eu/) to keep them up-to-date ([#1798](https://github.com/streetcomplete/StreetComplete/issues/1796))
