# Microgrant Contract

**Project Title:** Map Review Team

**Grantee:** Except One Limited (Tom Hukins, [@tomhukins](https://github.com/tomhukins/))

**Start Date:** January 2026

**Repository/Issue Link:** Not Applicable

**Proposal Link:** https://github.com/osmfoundation/ewg_bidding/issues/46

---

## 1. Scope of Work
The Grantee agrees to undertake the following tasks:

- HTML User Interface Discovery
- Integration (OSM API, WhoDidIt, OSM Teams)
- Web Application
- RSS Feeds
- Chat Notifications (optional)
- User Survey and Project Summary

Whether work takes place on _chat notifications_ and to what extent can
be discussed and agreed after the first milestone.

---

## 2. Timeline & Milestones
The Grantee commits to the following schedule:

- **Milestone 1:** Web Application — March 2026
- **Final Deliverable:** Completion — May 2026

Progress updates will be posted as comments on GitHub issue
[#46](https://github.com/osmfoundation/ewg_bidding/issues/46).

---

## 3. Reporting
- Grantee will provide short status updates at each milestone.
- Updates must include links to code, documentation, or other outputs.
- Issues or blockers should be flagged promptly.

---

## 4. Payment
- Payment is tied to milestone completion.
- Each milestone must be reviewed and approved by the Grant Committee.
- Upon approval, payment will be released according to the agreed schedule.

The OpenStreetMap Foundation will pay Except One Limited (Company number
08415317) in British Pounds (GBP).  VAT will be charged in addition to
the prices quoted:

- **Milestone 1:** Web Application — £2883
- **Final Deliverable:** Completion — (between £1572 and £2358)

These prices are based on the budget breakdown in Euros from the
original proposal at an exchange rate of 1 EUR to 0.87 GBP.

The varying price of the final deliverable depends on whether work takes
place on two, one, or no _chat notifications_ following agreement with
the Grant Committee.

---

## 5. Agreement
By accepting this contract, the Grantee agrees to:

- Deliver the work as described.
- Communicate progress openly via GitHub.
- Respect deadlines unless otherwise agreed.
